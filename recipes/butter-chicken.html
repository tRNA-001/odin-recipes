Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a classic Indian dish that's made by simmering marinated and grilled chicken in a spicy, aromatic, buttery, and creamy tomato sauce. This lip-smacking and delicious dish is hugely popular among the Indian food lovers across the world.

Ingredients

For marinate:

  • 1/2 lb chicken
  • yoghurt
  • cumin powder
  • garam masala
  • salt and pepper to taste

For sauce:

  • chopped onions
  • chopped garlic
  • chopped ginger
  • 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • unsalted butter
  • cilantro for garnish
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken for at least 30 minutes or overnight.

  2. After marinating, fry the chicken until half cooked. Remove chicken from the pan.

  3. Using the same pan, saute onion, garlic and ginger. Add crushed tomatoes. Add water on the 2/3 of the can of tomatoes and pour onto the pan. Let it simmer for 5-10 minutes.

  4. Add the fried chicken from earlier and simmer for 5-10 minutes.

  5. Add heavy cream and simmer for 3-5 minutes. Turn off heat. Then add butter and stir until fully melted. Add salt and pepper to taste if needed.

  6. Lastly, garnish with cilantro leaves.
